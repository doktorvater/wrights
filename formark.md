The five realms are part of the secret knowledge one gets in going up a level.

After earning three skill patches in rope, a compagnon learns that the secondary field of the group is impact.

After becoming a compagnon in impact, it is then learned that the realm also involves exhibitionism, leather, and parties for the Kinkwrights.

Similar to the other realms after rope, the realms that match up to tarot suits, compagnons in each realm must first pursue patches in the secondary elements of the realm before working on their own specializations.
