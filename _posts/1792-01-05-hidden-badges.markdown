Normal practice is to display the public badges in arriving at a hand event and removing the bag or clothing when the event starts, to reveal the kink achievements underneath. These skill badges may only be displayed at official Kinkwright events. 

The skill badges are to be displayed in columns of two badges side by side underneath the appropriate realm badge on the giving side on the left or the receiving side on the right. Each badge is chosen by the journeyperson when first committing to achieve a skill quest. The journeyperson may opt to have a realm badge different from the public badge she or he wears for that realm, but the unique realm badge must be approved along with the first skill badge for that realm when committing to the first skill quest.

Skill badges should be obvious, filthy, or funny. Knowing the realm the skill is in, members should be able to guess what sort of skill the badge symbolizes. 

Wearing of the badge must be approved at the meeting thirteen weeks or more after the commitment to work on the badge. Any badges that have not yet been earned one year after commitment must be approved again at the next meeting. This re-approval can only be done once before the commitment to the skill quest must be abandoned.

Non-members may never see the skill badges whether or not they are being worn. Only hand members may serve as witnesses of skill achievements, so consider nominating one of your play partners to membership in the hand if they do not belong to the group yet.
