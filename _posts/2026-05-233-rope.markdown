Rope represents the major arcana for this hand and therefore every member must first commit to earning the public badge for the realm of rope before anything else. This first rope badge can be worn on either side. 

Members are considered to be just apprentices until they earn their first rope badges. So, until a member displays a rope badge, she or he may only attend meetings to which a member has invited them. All journypersons who display a rope badge may attend any hand meeting. 

Their are six steps the must be earned in this order: 
1. Attend a rope classe that includes a discussion of safety in kink.
2. Write a post on the hand discord site where you describe how what you learned guides your practice in rope. This must be well thought out.
3. Research the different styles of rope and how they affect shibari. Purchase two hundred feet/sixty-five meters of your preferred rope and prepare the lengths you want from all it.
4. Practice the square knot, two single-column ties, and a double-column tie that uses a completely different knot. 
5. Select four tie patterns that include different types of harnesses and restraints. In total they should use all the knots you practiced. Find opportunities to practice your patterns on a person. Your first session of pattern practice must be observed by a hand journeyperson. And then after some practice, you must have two more sessions witnessed by a hand member in which you successfully tie all four patterns. 
6. Smoothly and swiftly display your four patterns correctly on a person while being witnessed by two separate people who are hand members with rope badges.

The two witness must be at the meeting in which the apprentice gets approval to rise to journeyperson status and where the rope badge.
