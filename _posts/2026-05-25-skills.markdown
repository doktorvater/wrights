Skill badges may be patches or pins. They must be selected in imitation of those worn by a least one compagnon of the hand or approved at your commitment meeting. Each badge acknowledges that you have earned recognition for learning kink skills. The badges generally start with a broad area and then get more specific, but you can earn multiple badges, such as getting a suspension badge and following that up with chest harnesses and then hip harnesses. Many compagnons follow their rope badge with a badge for floor work, one for suspension, and then perhaps one for rope restraint, before specializing in any particular knots or patterns. 

There are six steps the must be completed to earn a badge in a broad realm of skills: 
1. Attend a rope class specific to that activity which includes a discussion of how to safely avoid falls, nerve damage, and blood vessel damage.
2. Write a post on the hand discord site where you describe how what you learned about safety showed you special considerations for this type of kink and how that will guide your practice in rope. This must be well thought out.
3. Set up a space where you can regularly practice with other people in private, with all of the right equipment in place.
4. Practice four new patterns that you have not mastered before on a person. These should be central ties used often for your selected style, so you will need to research approved practices. 
5. Arrange to meet a badge-wearing mentor, as well as a hand compagnon you will tie, at three public rope events where you manage to complete all four of your patterns safely.
6. Smoothly and swiftly display your four patterns correctly on a person while being witnessed by two other people who are compagnon members of this hand with patches in the same style you are practicing.

There are six steps the must be completed to earn a pattern badge: 
1. Attend a rope class specific to that pattern taught by an experienced rope teacher you have never met.
2. Describe the pattern in detail, including illustrations, on the hand discord site. Make certain that you end the pattern with a personal twist that you have invented.
3. Practice the knots involved at least five times a week and complete the entire pattern at least another five times a week. Keep a record of your practice sessions and any problems that you had. 
4. Practice the complete pattern on another person three times in three consecutive weeks.
5. Convince at least one new person to attend a hand meeting and commit to pursuing the rope badge. 
6. Smoothly and swiftly display your pattern on another person in front of all the compagnons at the hand meeting where your badge is to be approved. 

Just as with the first badge, you must train for at least thirteen weeks and master the six steps before the badge can be approved. If a year has past since the commitment meeting, the members must vote to allow you to continue to pursue the badge. The hand only permits one such renewal.
