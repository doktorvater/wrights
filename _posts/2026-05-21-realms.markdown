The five realms for this Virginia group, or hand, of trainees include rope, impact, toys, obedience, and bondage. Other hands may focus on other practices. These realms are associated with specific colors, materials, secondary practices and a tarot suit. In training, the realms do no affect things very much, but they are used to provide suggestions for play and public events, as well as help in the interpretation of badges.

rope - white - hemp - sensation - cups

impact - black - leather - exhibitionism - major arcana

toys - purple - velcro - bisexuality - wands

obedience - red - patent leather - fire - pentacles

bondage - silver - steel - orgasm control - swords
