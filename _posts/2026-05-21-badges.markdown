If accepted as an apprentice, you must immediately commit at that meeting to earn the first rope badge and thereby advance to compagnon status in the hand. The rope badge you are attempting to earn should look the same as the rope badge worn by any other hand member or else be approved by the members at the meeting where you were accepted. The first badge should only make a visual reference to rope or knots, without suggesting sex or kink. As with the first badge, your choice of a skill badge different from any of the ones worn in your hand means that you must bring the badge to your commitment meeting and have it approved by a majority of those compagnons attending the meeting.

A compagnon can receive a badge for all levels and skills as either a giver or a receiver. This is to be represented by displaying a badge on the left side of the front of the body for experience gained in doing things, while the right side of the front of the body is for badges that show advancement in having kink done to you. The two aspects of the same skill will be represented by an identical badge on both sides of the chest. Differences in the type of activity may warrant different badges.

Badges are to be displayed publicly on the front of a jacket, shirt, or vest. Compagnons should wear their badges to any hand event. Identical badges to the ones you wear on your chest may also be attached to a bag used to bring kink equipment to any hand training or party, in the same layout as that used on your clothing. The bag and/or garment can also be brought to any classes or events that involve rope, where participants are not expected to share their legal names. Display of badges at any other type of public occassion may result in  a vote by the meeting of the hand to revoke those badges. Wearing any badge in public that suggests rope or kink without earning that badge from the hand will result in any apprentice or compagnon being banned from the group.

If you are at a meeting or other public event with another hand member to have that person witness any of your skill achievements, both of you must wear your Kinkwright rope badges into the event.
